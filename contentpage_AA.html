ACTIVE ACCOUNT



A US $41 billion corporation, the Aditya Birla Group is in the League of Fortune 500. It is anchored by an extraordinary force of over 120,000 employees, belonging to 42 different nationalities. Aditya Birla Financial Services wanted to develop a mobile application that would help your savings earn for you.



PROBLEM

Usually, everyone thinks twice while investing in a liquid fund. They also think it is time-consuming and taxing. The main problem was to make the interaction as easy as possible so that the users could invest the money quickly because Aditya Birla does the research for them about the liquid funds beforehand itself. They just need to select the scheme type to invest.

TARGET AUDIENCE

The app is targeted towards users in India within the age group of 22 years - 65 years with 78% being male users and the rest being female users. The user of the app can be anyone who wants to increase his savings, save tax as well as gives liquidity because its an open-ended fund.

CLIENT REQUIREMENT

The client requirements for the app were rather simple, the application needed to be simple witha clean design. It needed to motivate the user to invest. The interaction to invest and withdraw had to be simple. The only part that was text heavy in this application was the fact that the users needed to constantly update their FATCA form (Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act)

MARKET REQUIREMENTS


Our user research showed that there are 4 kinds of users:



Target Group 1: These are the users who have a PAN card/Aadhar card, are KYC compliant and already have a Folio with Aditya Birla.

Target Group 2: These are the users who have a PAN card/Aadhar Card, are KYC compliant but do not have a Folio with Aditya Birla.

Target Group 3: Users in this category do have a PAN card/Aadhar card but are not KYC compliant.

Target Group 4: These are the people who do not have a PAN Card/Aadhar card at all.
